# LILAM Enterprise & Commercial Licensing

LILAM is offered under a **Dual-Licensing Model** to support both the open-source community and professional enterprise requirements.

### 1. Open Source Edition (GPLv3)
Ideal for private projects, research, education, and pure open-source applications.
*   **License:** [GNU GPLv3](https://www.gnu.org)
*   **Condition:** If you integrate LILAM into your software and distribute it (externally or within a corporate group), the "Copyleft" clause of the GPLv3 requires you to disclose your own source code under the GPLv3 as well.
*   **Cost:** Free of charge.

### 2. Enterprise Edition (Commercial License)
Specifically designed for corporations, government agencies, and Independent Software Vendors (ISVs) who wish to utilize LILAM within their internal production processes or embed it into proprietary software architectures.
*   **The "GPL-Exemption":** With a commercial license, you are released from all obligations of the GPLv3, particularly the requirement to disclose your own proprietary source code.
*   **Your Benefits:** Legal Certainty (No "Viral Effect"), Proprietary Usage, and Priority Access to Enterprise Features (e.g., Dynamic Auto-Scaling).
